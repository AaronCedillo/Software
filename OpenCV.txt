Add OpenCV Paths in Qt
1.- In "Projects" option in Qt, select "Details" in "Build Enviroment".


2.- Find "path", here you can see something like this "C:\Users\".


3.- Here you need to paste the path where you have the OpenCV library. "C:\opencv\x86\vc10\bin" and "C:\opencv\x86\vc10\lib".


4.- "C:\opencv\x86\vc10\bin;C:\Users\". and in the end of the path ";C:\opencv\x86\vc12\bin;".
